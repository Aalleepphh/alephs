Sleep, weary traveler.
Be at ease, for you have ultimately arrived at your destination.
Do you know what you have come looking for?
Well, do not be alarmed, for I most certainly have what you seek.
And know that you are most welcome to take home anything you like,
I won't be worried for the contents of this library aren't even my own property.
Yea, they are gratuitous, as are the most worthful things in life.
Wander around and engorge yourself in my fine selections of art.
As for me, I will continue residing in my study, looking for more exemplary artpieces.
